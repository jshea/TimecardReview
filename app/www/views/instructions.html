Reviewed

  • Box is checked Timecard review completed or a Paper timecard has been submitted - Review completed and paper timecard indicator displayed under Follow up Action taken column. No action required
  • Box is unchecked Timecard review is not completed. Review is required
  • n/a is displayed Timecard is unavailable for review. No action is required

Follow-up Required

  • Box is available to be checked when you're unable to check the reviewed box until a discussion with the employee takes place.
  • Follow up Action Taken must be performed within 10 days of selecting the Follow up Required box.

Follow-up Action Taken

  • Follow up discussion with the employee has taken place.
  • Selection from the list of values must be performed to complete the review process.
